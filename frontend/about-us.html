About Us

Welcome to Mindsphere, your AI-powered mental health companion. Our mission is to make mental health support accessible, interactive, and effective for everyone.

Who We Are

Mindsphere is a passionate team of developers, designers, and mental health advocates working together to create a platform that helps individuals track their emotions, practice self-care, and connect with mental health professionals.

Our Mission

We believe that mental health is just as important as physical health. Through AI-driven insights, guided exercises, and emotional support, we aim to make a positive impact on mental well-being.
