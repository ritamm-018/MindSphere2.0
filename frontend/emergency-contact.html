Emergency Contacts

If you're in a mental health crisis, please contact one of the following emergency services:

  • National Suicide Prevention Hotline: 1-800-123-4567
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HELP to 741741
  • Local Mental Health Services: 1-123-456-7890
  • Friend/Family Contact: Call Family

Always reach out if you're in need of immediate help.
